Profile Completeness 70/100

  • Profile picture and cover photo are optimized
  • Contact information partially completed
  • Story highlights need organization and covers
  • Missing location tag on several platforms
RECOMMENDATION: Complete all contact fields across platforms. Create branded highlight covers with consistent design. Add location tags for local discovery boost.

Content Consistency 85/100

  • Strong posting frequency across all platforms
  • Consistent brand voice and visual identity
  • Content pillars well-defined and followed
  • Minor gaps in weekend posting schedule
RECOMMENDATION: Maintain current cadence. Fill weekend gaps with pre-scheduled evergreen content. Consider adding Sunday recap series.

Engagement Quality 60/100

  • Comment response rate below industry average
  • Limited use of interactive Stories features
  • DM engagement opportunities being missed
  • Community building efforts need improvement
RECOMMENDATION: Respond to top comments within 1 hour. Use polls, quizzes, and question stickers in every Story batch. Implement "Send This To..." CTAs to boost DM shares.

Discovery & Reach 75/100

  • Good use of trending audio in Reels
  • Hashtag strategy could be more targeted
  • Cross-platform sharing partially implemented
  • SEO optimization on YouTube is strong
RECOMMENDATION: Implement a tiered hashtag strategy (5 broad, 10 niche, 5 branded per post). Enable cross-posting automation for all platforms. Add alt text to all images.

Link & Conversion 65/100

  • Story link stickers used inconsistently
  • No tracking UTM parameters on shared links
  • Conversion funnel needs optimization
RECOMMENDATION: Update link-in-bio weekly with fresh content. Add UTM parameters to all external links. Use Story link stickers in every Story sequence. Track click-through rates.
